Our Ministries

Discover ways to connect, grow, and serve through our various ministry opportunities.

Disciplineship Training

Empowering believers through structured teachings and mentorship programs designed to deepen their understanding of the Gospel and equip them for ministry.

Campus Coordinators training

Providing leadership training and spiritual guidance to campus coordinators, empowering them to effectively lead and disciple students in their respective campuses.

Mentorship academy / Power Bible school

Providing in-depth biblical education and mentorship to equip believers with the knowledge and skills needed for effective ministry and personal spiritual growth.

school of ministry

Providing specialized training and resources for individuals called to ministry, equipping them with the tools and knowledge to effectively serve and lead in their communities.

Meet Our Pastor

Pastor Abel Damina

Senior Pastor

Pastor Abel damina has been faithfully serving our congregation for years, bringing wisdom, compassion, and biblical truth to every message. His heart for people and passion for God's Word has helped countless individuals discover their purpose and grow in their faith journey.

With a warm and approachable style, Pastor Abel Damina makes complex biblical concepts accessible to everyone, whether you're new to faith or have been walking with God for years.

Education: Master of Divinity from Theological Seminary

Years of Ministry: 20+ years of pastoral experience

Specialties: Biblical Teaching, Pastoral Care, Community Outreach
